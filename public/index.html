Become a
Blockchain Developer

WARNING: Before enbarking on this path you must first know that it will be a long ardous path. You will have times where it wont feel great, you will struggle, the computer screen will show incombrehesible clusters of letter and numbers. But after everytime time you stumble. YOU will have a better understanding of the concept. YOU will be one step closer to becoming a Blockchain Developer. Until one day YOU ARE A BLOCKCHAIN DEVELOPER

The concept of a Blockchain is relatively new in computer science. Just a little more than ten years ago Bitcoin did not even exist. With that, imagine trying to learn about something that does not yet exist. It requires imagination! Lucky for you with every month these new ecosystems and the development community within them are growing exponetially. This creates new material to learn from and on top of that an amazing opportunity to join welcoming communities and contribute to history. This website is an attempt to collect the material you will need, put in a easily accessible place and help you on your path to a new hobbie, career and future. It is set up in a way that even somebody with no computer knowledge will be directed to the right learning materials from start to finish ( You will soon learn as a developer there is no finish ). This is a dynamic website and is open to contiributions. Feel free to contact with tips, links, suggestions and collaborations. Together we will grow this space.

< Learn to code !>

WHOA!!! Hold up. You want to go straight to the meaty stuff don't you? Before you can move straight into building Blockchain architecture and dApps you will need a foundation. Building a solid foundation will only help cement you as the great developer you are going to be. If you have no coding experience it is essential you have a basic understanding of computer science principles like how a computer works, Binary and data, Algorithms and how the internet works. Then you can move on the fun with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. These are great as you begin your journey as they offer a fast visual feedback loop that will keep you interested enough to continue past the initial stumbling blocks.

Start with the history of how we got here and then go through the all the resources and media to get you up to speed with the present day situation.
