Become a
Blockchain Developer

One year ago I decided I wanted to position myself for the future and start learning how to code. Shortly after, I found Crypto and Blockchain technology. I'm not sure if it was dumb luck or if it was meant to be but from that moment I knew there was no way I could not possibly get involved. From then on I slowly transitioned from a six figure salary to the point now where I quit my job and am learning how to become a Blockchain Developer. I created this site to help others like myself find information, learn and transition into a new career designing the next layer of the internet. This page is currently under construction but if you would like to find out more information as it becomes available please fill in your details.
