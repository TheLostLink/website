The Fundamentals

“Give me six hours to chop down a tree and I will spend the first four sharpening the axe.”

- Abraham Lincoln

One of the most important parts to becoming a Blockchain Developer or any Developer for that matter is building a solid understanding of just how a computer works and also how it understands and interprets the language and commands given to it. As Johann Wolfgang von Goethe once put it "Everyone hears only what he understands". You will find the best resources below on common computer principles from basic to complex. For the novice, Computers often seem like magical boxes that are beyond comprehension. They can be complex but you will find that every process is can be broken down into steps. Every piece of computer code no matter how complex can ulimately be broken down into 1's and 0's. Once you know how to implement an algorithm and practice problem solving you will be the master of that dreaded 'black box' in front of you. Some of these concepts may not always sink in the first time but your persiverenes will set you apart from the others that fail. Below you will find everything you need to start your journey of taming the beast.
